Mirage Sea Serpent Scale

When wet, these scales glimmer with an otherworldly iridescent sheen. When dry, they appear like normal serpent scales. A small handful of these scales faintly glows.

HP Points/Inch: 2

Hardness: 1

Cost: 1 power component, 30 gp (worth of scales), potion 60 gp

Cost (Longer Wording): When you use this power component as a material component when casting an enchantment (compulsion) spell, increase the DC by 1. When crushed, it can be inhaled or mixed with a liquid and drank, the imbiber can breathe water freely for 14 hours. This does not make the creatures unable to breathe air.
