These mithral bracelets are simple but elegant bands that shrink or expand to fit around a willing creature's body. They can be interlocked together into one band with a standard action. Donning or doffing one individual band or both interlocked bands is a full-round action as the bands take time to adjust their size.

When separated, the bands prevent itself and those wearing it from leaving the plane they are on as the spell dimensional anchor, except that it also prevents summoned creatures from disappearing. Instead, if a summoned creature would disappear, they instead fall unconscious and cannot be made conscious again and disappear when the band is removed. If an eidolon wears a band, it can wear armor but still shares magic item slots with its summoner.

Touch spells can be cast through the bracelet to affect any wearer of a bound bracelet. Casting a touch spell though the bracelet worn by a creature unwilling to allow the spell requires a called shot to the arm with a -5 penalty. The bracelets can be used to cast accept afflication at will targeting a wearer of the bonded bracelet without requiring any checks or spell components.

Wearing the bracelets also grants abilities or bonuses depending on where the bracelet is worn and how many. Wearing a bracelet does take up a magic item slot.

The wearer or wearers of the bands can expend a use of mythic power to double the numerical bonuses of any one-band effects they are benefiting from for up to one hour. For the breath weapon, this effect doubles your effective dragon HD.

Others bonded can expend one use of mythic power to receive one of the active one-band effects as if they were wearing the band for up to one hour. Receiving an effect in this way does not take up a magic item slot.
