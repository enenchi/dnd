Sun-glasses

Item Creation Status

-1 DC on item creation tasks related to the logistics of using these as glasses.
-1 DC on most item creation tasks related to metaphysical stability and compatibility.

Qualities

Hardness 4
HP 8

The wearer gains the Eagle Eyes feat while looking through the sun-glasses.

A faint blur appears where there is an invisible creature, giving the wearer a +10 bonus to Perception to see invisible creatures and objects.

The wearer also becomes uncannily skilled at seeing Limgar. The wearer gains a +10 bonus to Perception to see Limgar and can make Perception checks to see Limgar even through total cover. If successful and Limgar is invisible or behind cover, the wearer sees a translucent image of Limgar overlaid where he is. Whether you see just Limgar (sans clothes) depends on if you conceptualize "Limgar" as just him or him and his equipment.

The sun-glasses are hard to see through, imposiving a -4 penalty to sight-based Perception checks as well as protecting the wearer from the effects of light sensitivity and light blindess due to sunlight, the daylight spell, or similar light effects.

Looking through the sunglasses inside a vision allows the wearer to see the real location of the vision if it exists and the wearer is close to it.

Desciption

A pair of glasses with a silvery-golden frame and green-ish purple lenses. Difficult to see through, but provides good protection against the sun. Attuned to the aspect of introspection and adaptation.
